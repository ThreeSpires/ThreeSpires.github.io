Three Spires United is an amateur football club based in staffordshire. Currently playing in the Staffordshire County Senior League.

Improve:

Physical Health

Our club provides exciting, enjoyable & competitive opportunities to improve fitness which leads to players gaining good exercising habits

Mental Health & Wellbeing

As a club we want to form a community of support, while providing an outlet for every player to express themselves.

Excel:

As a club we are passionate about developing every member’s abilities to perform at their very best. We’re all winners at heart and we believe we can cultivate a winning squad by nurturing individuals to their true potential.

Respect:

Respect for every player, coach & spectator is a core, fundamental value of our club. This is integrated into the culture of the club and is strictly upheld.

Include:

Our Club is inclusive to all with a value for social diversity and providing equal opportunities to all members.
